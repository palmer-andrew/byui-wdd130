More Than Just The Convenience

Andrew's Orchard, of Brandon, Manitoba, Canada, is the perfect destination for local health-conscious food lovers who want to enjoy fresh veggies, fruit and more while also promoting good environmental stewardship and organic practices. We offer a wide variety of vegetables and fruits that are grown locally with sustainable and natural farming methods, ensuring consumers get a local choice for the best quality produce. So whether you're looking for something to snack on or a full meal of vegetables and fruit, we have it all - even free range eggs!

Fresh Fruit

The fruit that we offer at Andrew's Orchard is fresh, juicy and delicious. We have a wide selection of fruit available, such as apples, pears, strawberries and blueberries. All of our fruit is grown using organic practices, which means no harmful chemicals or chemical fertilizers are used in the growing process. We also use sustainable methods to ensure that our fruit is harvested in its peak season for maximum flavor and nutrition. Our fruit comes straight from the farm to your table - full of vitality and flavors! Come visit us today to sample some of Manitoba's finest fruit!
