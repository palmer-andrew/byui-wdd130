Overview

Purpose

Andrew's Orchard, of Brandon, Manitoba, Canada, is the perfect destination for local health-conscious food lovers who want to enjoy fresh veggies, fruit and more while also promoting good environmental stewardship and organic practices. We offer a wide variety of vegetables and fruits that are grown locally with sustainable and natural farming methods, ensuring consumers get a local choice for the best quality produce. Whether a passerby is looking for something to snack on or a full meal of vegetables and fruit, we have it all! We also have free range eggs, recommended recipes and more to round out the offerings.

Audience

With vegetables and fruits, Andrew's Orchard is an ideal destination for those who want to get their daily dose of vegetables, fruit and other local goodies. As well, people looking for a more sustainable lifestyle will appreciate our natural growing methods while knowing they're doing their part for the environment. Our clients can look forward to a wide selection of vegetables and fruit, from cucumbers and tomatoes to apples and pears – something for everyone.
