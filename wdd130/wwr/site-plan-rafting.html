Overview

Purpose

At Northern Skies, whitewater is our passion! We are committed to growing the whitewater community and introducing whitewater-rafting to new audiences. Our mission is twofold: to bring together whitewater enthusiasts from around the world and to create positive whitewater experiences for those just getting started.

Audience

We host whitewater adventures for all skill levels and ages, from the beginner to the expert, from couples to groups. Our whitewater trips are designed to help you make new friends and take your whitewater skills to the next level. With our easy-to-use website and mobile app, we make it easy for you to find whitewater trips that fit your budget and interests.
